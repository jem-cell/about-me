Selected work

A few public projects.

Things I've shipped, or am working on. Most live on GitHub; the rest are private client work.

  1. Survey Air · drone ops 2025–present

    Survey Air — internal tooling

    Dashboards, AI agents and batch-processing scripts for a three-person drone startup. Built to cut the manual time on photogrammetry and LiDAR pipelines, centralise project reporting, and route routine client interactions through an automated agent.

    Private — employed work, not on GitHub.

  2. Aerodynamics · dissertation 2019

    F1 wing-endplate aerodynamics

    Self-defined undergraduate dissertation. Designed two wing-endplate specifications against FIA Formula One regulations and ran them through computational and wind-tunnel analysis to produce a full aerodynamic comparison. Available on request.

    Dissertation, Swansea University — available on request.

  3. Next.js · TypeScript 2026

    Health Optimizer Dashboard

    A dark-themed web app that pulls in Google Fit vitals, calculates macro targets for cut/bulk phases, and serves AI-generated insights. The interesting part is wiring OAuth, a third-party health API and an LLM behind one minimal UI — and shipping it on Next.js + TypeScript.

    github.com/jem-cell/Health-app →

  4. Three.js · vanilla 2025

    3D Georeference

    A browser tool for placing point clouds and image-derived markers in 3D space over real-world coordinates. I built it to learn Three.js properly, and because the existing tools were all desktop-only.

    github.com/jem-cell/3D_Georeference →

  5. Leaflet · vanilla 2025

    GeoMapper

    Image-metadata visualizer — drop a folder of photos and it reads the EXIF/GPS data and plots them on a Leaflet map. Small, fast, useful. The companion piece to 3D Georeference for the 2D case.

    github.com/jem-cell/georeference →
